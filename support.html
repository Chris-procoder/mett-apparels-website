Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a custom garment?

Typically, our custom garments take 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity of the design and current workload. We'll provide you with an estimated completion date during your consultation.

Do you offer alterations for garments not made by you?

Yes, we provide professional alteration services for garments from other tailors or off-the-rack clothing. Our skilled tailors can help achieve the perfect fit.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations. For custom orders, cancellations are accepted within 48 hours of placing the order, before work begins.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options for custom orders over $500. A 50% deposit is required to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.

What if I'm not satisfied with my garment?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. We offer complimentary adjustments within 30 days of completion to ensure the perfect fit and your complete satisfaction.
