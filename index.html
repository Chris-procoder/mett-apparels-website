Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Moselyn Junius, Mett-Apperals has been dedicated to the art of fine tailoring. Our master tailors combine traditional techniques with modern design to create garments of exceptional quality and style.

We believe that clothing should be as unique as the individual wearing it. That's why we take the time to understand your personal style and requirements, ensuring that every piece we create is a perfect reflection of you.
